Hi, I'm Mariana

I am very passionate about the close relationship between architecture theory and its practical implementation in the real world. The architect remaining a lifelong learner, not only of cities, but of human life is something I’m very interested in.

I am motivated and a curious life-long learner. Having worked in various cultural backgrounds has provided me a rich perspective in handling challenges. I work well both in a team and independently and often can use my own initiative when meeting tight deadlines.

I have had opportunities to work in different locations (Europe, Asia and Africa) during my studies. I believe this was important in my formation as an architect and allows me varied perspectives.
